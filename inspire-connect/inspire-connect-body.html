Inspire Connect offers services for mapping organisational culture, boosting employee engagement, obtaining recognised certifications, assessing staff well-being, and developing strategies to enhance communication and support sustainability goals.

Every business depends on its people. Therefore, Inspire focuses on employee well-being programmes that are set up for long-term success. It’s important to have healthy and happy employees to create a strong company. This is your first step to fostering employee well-being programmes. Inspire Connect is key to making that happen!

  • Comprehensive Surveys: Our unique Culture Map structurally charts your organisational culture by listening, planning, implementing, measuring, and redesigning throughout the journey. We ensure a deep understanding of cultural dynamics and facilitate continuous improvement.
  • Brand Certification: This comprehensive workplace culture analysis and certification platform enhances employee engagement, improves organisational communication, and strengthens employer branding. It also supports the achievement of recognised certifications, including WELL, GPTW, Gallup, Parents Label, and Inclusive HR.
  • Culture Audit: Our culture audit uses focus groups and pulse surveys to conduct in-depth assessments of organisational culture, providing valuable insights into employee health and well-being. This empowers organisations to make informed decisions that enhance workplace culture and drive engagement.
  • Communication Strategy: This approach improves internal and external communication practices, fosters open dialogue, reduces communication waste, and supports the company’s sustainability goals.
